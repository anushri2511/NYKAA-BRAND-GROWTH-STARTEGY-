# 💄 Nykaa Instagram Growth Strategy & Analytics Dashboard


## 📌 Project Overview

This project presents a complete **Instagram Growth Strategy for Nykaa**, combining marketing analysis, competitor benchmarking, content planning, and performance tracking through an interactive **Power BI Dashboard**.

The objective was to identify growth opportunities, improve engagement, and design a data-driven social media strategy capable of increasing Instagram engagement and follower growth by **20% within 3 months**. :contentReference[oaicite:0]{index=0}

---

## 🎯 Business Objective

> How can Nykaa increase Instagram engagement and follower growth by 20% over the next 3 months? :contentReference[oaicite:1]{index=1}

To answer this question, the project follows a structured marketing framework:

✅ Instagram Audit  
✅ Competitor Analysis  
✅ Problem Identification  
✅ Growth Strategy Development  
✅ Content Calendar Planning  
✅ KPI Dashboard Creation

- Engagement performance

- Reels effectiveness

## 🚨 Key Problems Identified

After the audit, the following gaps were discovered:

🔸 Excessive promotional content

🔸 Limited educational and value-driven reels

🔸 Lower influencer-led engagement compared to competitors

🔸 Missed opportunities for community-generated content

:contentReference[oaicite:5]{index=5}

## 🚀 Growth Strategy

A content strategy was designed around four high-impact content pillars:

### 🎨 Content Pillars

#### 💡 Beauty Tips
Educational skincare and beauty advice

#### 📚 Product Education
Product usage tutorials and demonstrations

#### 🤳 Influencer Reviews
Creator-led content and testimonials

#### ❤️ User Generated Content (UGC)
Community engagement and customer experiences
